**Subject:** Satellite Imagery Access Request — Humanitarian Tech Research / Personal Safety Systems

Dear Maxar Technologies Commercial Team,

SkyRights Foundation is a Belgian non-profit organization (ASBL, in process of registration)
developing satellite-based technology to guarantee fundamental human rights to people without
recognized identity — with an estimated target population of 800 million individuals worldwide.

We are writing to request access to **WorldView imagery** for initial feasibility testing of
two interconnected systems currently in technical development:

---

### System 1 — SkyID: Universal Digital Identity

A biometric identity system for stateless persons using:
- Gesture recognition (MediaPipe, on-device)
- Starlink direct-to-cell satellite connectivity
- Biometric hashing (SHA-256 of facial embedding — never raw face data)
- Blockchain registration (Polygon)

**Satellite use case:** Verification of location and timing for identity registration
in areas without ground infrastructure. High-resolution imagery used for:
- Ground truth verification of GPS coordinates
- Environmental context for identity registration events
- Post-registration audit trail (non-biometric)

**Precedent:** Aadhaar (India) — $1.08/person for 1.4 billion identities.
SkyID targets ~$4/person for 800 million people.

---

### System 2 — Personal Protection Protocol (Protocollo Scudo)

An autonomous personal safety system activated by a specific gesture,
which triggers automatic satellite tasking over the subject's GPS position.

**Satellite use case:**
- Automated tasking on GPS coordinates within 2-10 minutes of trigger activation
- 30-50cm resolution imagery of a ~500m radius around the subject
- Documentation of the area for legal/security purposes
- No facial recognition or surveillance of third parties

**Test parameters we need:**
- 1-3 test taskings in controlled environments (urban and non-urban)
- Resolution: 30-50cm (WorldView-2 or WorldView-3)
- Delivery: via API within 5-30 minutes of tasking request
- Format: GeoTIFF or JPEG with GPS metadata

---

### Why Maxar WorldView

WorldView-3 offers the commercial benchmark we need:
- 30cm resolution: sufficient to document presence and context
- API access: enables autonomous tasking (no manual operator)
- Response time: compatible with real-time personal protection scenarios
- Commercial availability: no government authorization required
- Legal framework: standard commercial license applicable

---

### Proposed Collaboration Structure

**Phase 1 (Testing — 3 months):**
- Limited access: 5-10 tasking requests for technical validation
- Reporting: full technical report shared with Maxar
- Compensation: standard commercial rate or humanitarian discount

**Phase 2 (Pilot — 6-12 months):**
- Integration with SkyID system
- Field test in partnership with humanitarian organization (target: UNHCR)
- Evaluation of response time for real-world personal protection scenarios

**Phase 3 (Scale):**
- Commercial license for full deployment
- Potential co-development of automated tasking API for humanitarian use
